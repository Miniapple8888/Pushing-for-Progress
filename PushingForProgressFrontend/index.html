Feeling down?

The Impact of COVID

As COVID-19 hit, multiple facets of our lives have been modified. Technology has become a life essential tool for the subsistance of the economy as well as a replacement for communication. On the negative side of the remote environment, a lot of people report feelings of loss of motivation to perform well at work and an overall decrease of their mental health. We seeked to address this issue by creating an app which can help people keep in touch with themselves by helping them build healthy habits such as sleeping well, meditating, eating regularly, doing fitness excercise, and so on.

Features
Push-notifications right to your desktop to remind you to eat, sleep, exercise, and meditate!
Meditate with our app as it teaches you the proper technique to breath!
Keep track of your productivity with our insightful analysis ofs your day!
